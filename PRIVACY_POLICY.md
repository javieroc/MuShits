# Privacy Policy for MuShits

**Effective Date:** March 30, 2026

MuShits ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Location Information
To provide you with local weather forecasts, the App requests access to your device's precise (GPS) and coarse location. This information is sent to third-party weather service providers to retrieve current weather data for your area.

### b. Media and Audio Files
The App requires access to your device's audio files (`READ_MEDIA_AUDIO`) to allow you to browse and play music stored on your device. We do not upload your music files to any external servers.

### c. Local Preferences and Usage Data
We store certain preferences and application state data locally on your device using Android Jetpack DataStore. This includes:
* **UI Preferences:** Such as your selected color mode (e.g., Monochrome, Green, White).
* **Playback State:** The ID of the last song played and your playback position, so you can resume listening where you left off.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
* Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (music playback and weather display).
* Personalize your experience by remembering your UI preferences.
* Improve the App's performance and user interface.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App interacts with the following third-party services:

* **Weather API Providers:** We send your location coordinates to a weather API (such as Open-Meteo) to fetch local weather data. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle location data.
* **Unsplash API:** The App may fetch background images from Unsplash. While we do not share your personal data with Unsplash, your device's interaction with their servers is subject to their privacy policy.

## 4. Data Retention and Security
All user preferences and playback history are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a central database of user information. We take reasonable measures to protect any data processed by the App, but no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Permissions
The App requests the following permissions:
* **INTERNET:** To fetch weather data and background images.
* **ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION / ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION:** To provide local weather updates.
* **READ_MEDIA_AUDIO:** To play your local music files.
* **FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK:** To allow music to continue playing while the App is in the background.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
